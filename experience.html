Selleck Chemicals LLC

Selleck Chemicals LLC is the world's leading supplier of small molecule inhibitors and modulators. Selleck has thousands products which consist of inhibitors, therapeutic antibodies, proteins, peptides, and natural products which focus on nearly every signaling pathways such as JAK/STAT, PI3K/Akt/mTOR, and other targets in epigenetics, autophagy, apoptosis etc..

Selleck specializes in high through compound libraries including the FDA-approved, Pfizer licensed, kinase inhibitor, drug repurposing and much more. Investigators can choose a library consisting of inhibitors from a specific pathway or any research area. Selleck will provide assistance in a custom Cherry-Pick Library in a variety of sizes.
